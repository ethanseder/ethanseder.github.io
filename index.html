Projects

Bike Safety Airbag

I am developing an airbag for bicyclists. The system is designed to be integrated into the shoulder straps of the user’s backpack. Using 9-axis IMU's and an Arduino controller, the system senses impending impacts and deploys an airbag. The airbag is designed to protect the upper chest and the lower half of the face. The algorithm, developed using Unity3d software and supported by real world analysis, was tested against simulated accidents and was refined to reduce false deployments.

I have been a part of Harvard’s SEAS racing team for two years. We are building a fuel-efficient vehicle to compete in the SAE Super-mileage Competition.
